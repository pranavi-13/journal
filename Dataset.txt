Rice Leaf Dataset:

1) https://www.kaggle.com/vbookshelf/rice-leaf-diseases\
(small dataset; Disease detected in this datset: Bacterial leaf blight,brown spot,leaf smut)

2) https://www.kaggle.com/minhhuy2810/rice-diseases-image-dataset
(Diseases detected in this dataset: brown spot,hispa,leafblast- 2000 images dataset)

3) https://www.kaggle.com/shayanriyaz/riceleafs
(Contains 3355 images of BrownSpot, Healthy, Hispa, LeafBlast) 

4)https://github.com/aldrin233/RiceDiseases-DataSet
(bacterial leaf blight, blast, brownspot. Smaller dataset, 80-100 images per disease)

5) https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/rpck298r8b/1

Detecting disease in plants:

https://medium.com/analytics-vidhya/plants-doctor-detecting-diseases-in-plants-e5c44c569bdb
Dataset: https://github.com/spMohanty/PlantVillage-Dataset
(huge dataset) 
Description: It contains images of 17 fungal diseases, 4 bacterial diseases, 2 molds (oomycete) diseases, 2 viral diseases, and 1 disease caused by a mite. 12 crop species also have images of healthy leaves that are not visibly affected by a disease. It has 38 classes of diseases.
